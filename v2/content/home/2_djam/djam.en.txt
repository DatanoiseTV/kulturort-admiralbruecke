Title: D-Jam

----

Heading: D-Jam: Fourteen nations,

----

Heading-accent: one rhythm.

----

Slogan: An open jam. All ages, all levels, all languages. Bring an instrument – or just yourself.

----

Text:

<p>The D-Jam is communal music-making at moderate volume that draws in everyone around – through listening, joining in, making contact, dancing, enjoying. Musicians from fourteen nations, from East and West, North and South, come together here regularly to experience community and carry liveliness into the world.</p>
<p>And that in times of war, of social hardening, of social impoverishment. One thing has become clear: music is everyone's language, a way for people to connect – directly, humanly, respectfully.</p>

----

Features: all ages / moderate volume / open to everyone / handmade music
